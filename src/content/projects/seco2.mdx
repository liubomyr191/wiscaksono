## Seco2
Seco2 is a carbon footprint calculator for film and audiovisual productions. It is homologated by the CNC (Centre National du Cinéma et de l'Image Animée), the French national agency for cinema and the moving image. Seco2 allows producers of film and audiovisual works to calculate the carbon footprint of their productions and to take steps to reduce it. The website provides a step-by-step guide to calculating the carbon footprint of a production, from the conception of the project to the distribution of the finished work. It also includes a database of carbon emission factors for a wide range of activities, such as transportation, filming, and post-production. Seco2 is a valuable tool for producers who want to reduce the environmental impact of their work. It is also a requirement for producers who want to receive funding from the CNC. 
[Preview](https://www.seco2.fr/).

### Features
- Carbon footprint calculator: Seco2 provides a step-by-step guide to calculating the carbon footprint of a production.
- Database of carbon emission factors: Seco2 includes a database of carbon emission factors for a wide range of activities, such as transportation, filming, and post-production.
- Reporting tool: Seco2 provides a reporting tool that allows producers to track their progress in reducing the carbon footprint of their productions.
- Certification: Seco2 is homologated by the CNC, which means that it meets the requirements of the French government for carbon footprint calculators.
