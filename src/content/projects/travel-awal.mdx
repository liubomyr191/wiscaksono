## Travel Awal
Travelawal is a travel planning website that helps users find the best deals on flights, hotels, and other travel expenses. The website also offers a variety of travel guides and articles to help users plan their trips. The website is easy to use and navigate. Users can search for flights and hotels by destination, date, and price. The website also allows users to compare prices from different travel agencies. Travelawal also offers a variety of travel guides and articles. These guides provide information on popular tourist destinations, as well as lesser-known gems. The articles cover a variety of topics, such as travel tips, packing lists, and cultural insights. Overall, Travelawal is a comprehensive travel planning website that can help users save money and plan their trips with ease.
[Preview](https://travelawal.vercel.app/).
