## Clevis
Clevis is a cutting-edge mobile application that revolutionizes the way you handle everyday chores, making laundry and car repairs a breeze. Powered by React Native and enhanced with the sleek design of Tailwind CSS, Clevis offers a seamless and user-friendly experience for ordering, tracking, and managing these essential services from the palm of your hand.
[Preview](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.alawaelsky.clevis&pcampaignid=web_share)

### Features
- Order and Schedule: Easily place laundry orders and schedule pickup and delivery times at your convenience.",
- Service Variety: Select from a range of laundry services, from regular washing to special treatments.",
- Real-time Tracking: Monitor the status of your laundry, from pickup to delivery, on an interactive map.",
- Request Repairs: Submit car repair requests, detailing the issue and your location.",
- Service Options: Choose from a variety of car repair and maintenance services.",
- Expert Mechanics: Access a network of skilled mechanics who come to your location for hassle-free repairs.",
- Live Updates: Track the rider's location in real-time as they approach your pickup or delivery location.",
- Transparency: Stay informed about the estimated time of arrival and any delays.",
- User-Friendly Interface: Enjoy a clean and user-friendly interface for easy navigation.",
- Customization: Tailor your service preferences and settings to suit your needs."

### Stack
- [React](https://nextjs.org) - Next.js is a React framework for building full-stack web applications
- [React Native](https://reactnative.dev/) - React Native is an open-source UI software framework created by Meta Platforms, Inc.
- [TailwindCSS](https://tailwindcss.com) - A utility-first CSS framework packed with classes that can be composed to build any design, directly in your markup.
- [Expo](https://expo.dev) - Expo is a toolchain based on React Native that will help you set up applications quickly.
